Skin Type Survey for Foreigners

Part 1. Oil (O) vs Dry (D)

After washing your face and applying nothing, does your face feel tight?

How much oil usually appears on your nose or forehead (T-zone)?

When you look in the mirror, what is the size of your pores?

In the afternoon, does your makeup smudge or clump due to oil?

Do you often experience dead skin cells on your face?

Part 2. Sensitive (S) vs Resistant (R)

When you change cosmetics, do you experience skin troubles or itching?

Does your face turn red when you drink alcohol or eat spicy food?

Does your skin sting after only 20 minutes in the sun without sunscreen?

Do you have visible capillaries or redness on your face?

Have you ever suffered from skin conditions such as atopy or seborrheic dermatitis?

Part 3. Pigmented (P) vs Non-Pigmented (N)

Do brown marks always remain after acne or wounds?

Do you have noticeable blemishes, freckles, or spots on your face?

Do you feel that spots are increasing compared to the past?

When exposed to sunlight, does your skin turn dark immediately rather than red?

Is the skin color on your upper lip or cheekbones unbalanced?

Part 4. Wrinkles (W) vs Firmness (T)

Do you usually smoke or are you frequently exposed to passive smoking?

When you smile, do fine lines remain around your eyes or mouth?

Do you apply sunscreen daily (including indoors)?

If a pillow mark appears on your face, does it disappear quickly?

Do you feel that your skin is losing elasticity and sagging?
